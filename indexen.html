About us

At Rawabi Baladna, we are passionate about preserving the authentic taste of traditional Arabic dairy products. Based in the heart of Jordan, our family-owned business takes pride in producing high-quality cheese, laban, and labneh using only the freshest ingredients. Our dairy products are made from our own milk, sourced from locally raised cows, ensuring the highest level of quality, sustainability, and taste. With a deep commitment to supporting local farmers and honoring age-old traditions, we bring you the purest flavors of the Middle East, crafted with care and respect for the land we call home.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become a trusted leader in the Arabic dairy industry, known for our commitment to quality, sustainability, and community. We aspire to expand our reach beyond local markets, sharing the rich flavors of traditional dairy with the world while maintaining our strong roots in local agriculture and artisanal craftsmanship.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide our community with authentic, high-quality Arabic dairy products made from locally sourced ingredients and our own fresh milk. We are dedicated to preserving traditional methods of dairy production while promoting sustainability, supporting local farmers, and delivering a wholesome, delicious product that brings people together.
